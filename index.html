Film Music Score

The Film Music Score is a film score company based in Italy. Has been founded to create collaborations with composers, sound designer and film studios. Is also a home to a group of composer around the world mentored by Nicola Lerra. The Film Music Score has been responsible for the scores for a number of successful short films, TV and music productions including My Grandfather Was a Cherry Tree (Short), The Amazing Spider-man 2 – Rise of Electro (Music Productions of the The New Era cover remix – composed by Nicola Lerra ) , In The Name of a God (Short) , A Special Day (Short) , It Could Be You (Short) , Every Man has a Price (Short) , Believe ( Music Productions – soundtrack composed by Nicola Lerra and winner an Hollywood Music Awards).
